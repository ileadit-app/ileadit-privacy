Privacy Policy for Ileadit

Last updated: January 27, 2025

Introduction

Ileadit is a mobile application owned and operated by Kranthews Ltd. At Ileadit, we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information (email address, name) when you create an account
  • Health and fitness data through Google Fit integration, including:
    • Step count
    • Distance traveled
    • Calories burned
    • Heart rate data
    • Activity segments

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Track your fitness progress
  • Enable competition features with other users
  • Send important updates about the app

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Your health and fitness data is stored securely and is not shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Fit API to collect fitness data. By using our app, you also agree to Google's Privacy Policy when using Google Fit services.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data collection

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
